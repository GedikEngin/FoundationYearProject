The City of Manchester

Manchester is a major city in the northwest of England with a rich industrial heritage.

Manchester city centre is jam-packed with unique and eclectic restaurants, bars, shops, museums, galleries, hotels and places to stay.

The City

A city with many different landmarks, from historical landmarks to sports stadiums, there is a wide variety of landmarks that the city has to offer.

Manchester cathedral

One of the most famous landmarks in Manchester, known for its architecture and history within housing WWII paintings and even stories of sarcophagus.

Football Stadiums

Football is big within Manchester, housing two top tier football clubs in Manchester United and Manchester City. Both stadiums Are iconic landmarks for the city-Old Trafford and The Etihad Stadium.

Facts About the city

Manchester is a city that has a past steeped in history and therefore has many interesting facts about it. Some notable ones are:
-Manchester started the industrial revolution when they opened the Bridgewater Canal in 1761, allowing Manchester to become the biggest stopping point for the textile industry.
-Famous bands are from Manchester. Some notable ones are: Oasis, Stone Roses and The Smiths.
