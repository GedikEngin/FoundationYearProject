The City of Manchester

Manchester is a major city in the northwest of England with a rich industrial heritage.

Manchester city centre is jam-packed with unique and eclectic restaurants, bars, shops, museums, galleries, hotels and places to stay.

Visit the city website

The City

A city with many different landmarks, from historical landmarks to sports stadiums, there is a wide variety of landmarks that the city has to offer.

Manchester cathedral

One of the most famous landmarks in Manchester, known for its architecture and history within housing WWII paintings and even stories of sarcophagus.

Football Stadiums

Football is big within Manchester, housing two top tier football clubs in Manchester United and Manchester City. Both stadiums Are iconic landmarks for the city-Old Trafford and The Etihad Stadium.

Facts About the city

Manchester is a city that has a past steeped in history and therefore has many interesting facts about it. Some notable ones are:
-Manchester started the industrial revolution when they opened the Bridgewater Canal in 1761, allowing Manchester to become the biggest stopping point for the textile industry.
-Famous bands are from Manchester. Some notable ones are: Oasis, Stone Roses and The Smiths.

The Culture

Manchester the city witnessed it's success during the industrial revolution in the 19th century-shaping the city's pioneering attitude. Presently, the city of Manchester has many successes within the sports, music and artistic scenes

The Food

The city offers a wide variety of food and drink that comes from many different countries globally. Known for it's second biggest Chinatown only to Manchester and the renowned 'Curry Mile' there is cuisine from almost every culture within the city.

Sports

Within the city, sports is a big part of the culture, specifically football. Hosting two of the biggest football clubs in the world presently in Manchester United and Manchester City. Alongside this, Manchester is also the host to many national centres of different sports like cycling, taekwondo and water polo.

Night Life

Manchester is one of the most well known cities in the UK for its music and nightlife. There are many different types of bars and clubs within Manchester. From Factory (a club known for indie music and bass type music) to the Gay Village known for its strong LGBT friendly area, there is usually a club in Manchester that suits your tastes
